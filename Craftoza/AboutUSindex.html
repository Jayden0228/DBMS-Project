Our beginning...

The contemporary world has prompted businesses to expand exponentially. Online software applications have provided a revolutionary way to grow and expand business worldwide. Centering around this fact, we studied that local businesses can also be a part of this massive opportunity. Being a native of the beautiful land of Goa, we feel we can contribute in promoting Goa's peculiar handicraft industry by elevating local and small-scale business artisans to the online platform.
